About Me

My name is Aidan Mellin. I am twenty-three, and I originally began coding at twelve, taking tech classes at a nonprofit in downtown Baltimore City. The logical progression from programming as a hobby to a profession—alongside a major in Computer Science and a minor in psychology—has suited me well. I love learning and try to do so daily, whether at work or in my free time. Though I don’t have nearly as much time for personal projects anymore, I still have a list of projects I’d like to learn more about and old projects I’d love to refactor with the new things I’ve discovered. Other uses of my free time include hiking, reading, picking up a new hobby, and trying something new. I am always looking for ways to grow both professionally and personally, and I have found the most success in the intersection of the two.
